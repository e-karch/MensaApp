chicco di caffè Karlsruhe

Coffee specialities for the break!

The coffee bar of chicco di caffè is located directly in the main entrance of the Studentenhaus. Here we are happy to prepare classic coffee specialities for you, as well as drinking chocolates, high-quality teas and chai latte teas. To spice up our beverage creations, we have a selection of high-quality syrups and fine coffee spices ready for you.

We also spoil you daily with special daily offers, which - like all our drinks - you can enjoy on the spot or simply take away in a cup.

We prepare all our coffee drinks in the classic way using a filter holder machine and always with freshly ground espresso. We pay attention to the best origin of the green coffee, which we roast ourselves in our own roastery using the traditional handicraft drum roasting method. Our espresso beans and coffees are also CO2-neutral. All the ingredients of our speciality coffees can be 100 percent traced and named. Our coffees are first roasted individually, then thoroughly blended and their taste perfected in numerous tastings.

During the summer months, you can also enjoy our delicious crushed ice coffee specialities in the Studentenhaus.
